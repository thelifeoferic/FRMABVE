Plant Bar Brand Notes

Core feel
- Warm, botanical, intentional, crafted.
- Specialty coffee, tea, boba, and zero-proof drinks built around mood, connection, and intention.
- Editorial cafe energy, not loud nightlife or cannabis retail.

Color direction
- Soft mint: #e5f5ef
- Blush pink: #ffa2a8
- Deep navy: #061f33
- Cocoa brown: #704f49
- Tea amber: #d9a45f
- Warm cream: #f4efe6

Typography direction
- Primary: bold Archivo-style sans for clear labels and UI.
- Editorial accent: Scotch-style serif for refined, premium moments.
- Script accent: warm hand-script energy for short mood statements only.

Image direction
- Use cafe light, glassware, garnish, drink texture, botanical details, hands, friends, and calm gathering.
- Keep layouts airy and intentional with breathing room.
- Avoid heavy black packaging, neon lounge styling, cannabis dispensary cues, or loud sales graphics.

Logo use
- Use the pink Plant Bar wordmark on mint, cream, or dark navy.
- Keep logo placement restrained and premium.
