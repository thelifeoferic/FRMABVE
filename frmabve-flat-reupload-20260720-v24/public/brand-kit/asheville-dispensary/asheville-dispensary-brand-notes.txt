Asheville Dispensary Brand Notes

Core feel
- Cannabis, apothecary, and lounge.
- Premium, local, natural, restrained, product-forward.

Color direction
- Cream: #f8f4ec
- Black: #171717
- Deep green: #1f3f21
- Moss: #315f34
- Warm gold: #c9a24a
- Cherry accent: #671f34

Typography direction
- Primary brand font: Iskra W01.
- Supporting brand font: Montserrat.
- Use Iskra selectively for expressive brand headings and short display moments.
- Use Montserrat for clean campaign fields, body copy, buttons, and utility text.
- Keep copy compact and confident.

Image direction
- Product-forward, warm cream surfaces, soft daylight, natural shadows, fruit or botanical cues.
- Keep product packaging readable and fully inside the frame.
- Avoid cluttered collage layouts and oversized text that risks cropping.

Sender defaults
- From email: no-reply@avldispensary.com
- Reply-to email: support@avldispensary.com
